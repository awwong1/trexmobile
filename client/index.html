About Us

As a company, our goal is to provide our customers with an alternative way to promoting their business. The idea first started when we were faced with the challenge to try and increase the awareness of our company in a way that could reach everyone, not just those who browse the internet. Now, with this new idea, we moved forward and made it into something other customers could use as well. We have now given our customers more flexibility to promoting their business and increasing the awareness to the company and their customer horizon. Not only is this product mobile, and efficient but also environmentally friendly.
